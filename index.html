15 Minutes Ago

Sajek Valley

Sajek valley is known for its natural environment and surrounded by mountains, dense forest, and grasslands hill tracks. Many small rivers flow through the mountains among which Kachalong and Machalong are notable.

15 Minutes Ago

Cox's Bazar

The Cox’s Bazar sea-beach is the longest unbroken sandy sea-beach in the world. It may be mentioned here that Cox’s Bazar has been named after Captain Heraim Cox. The whole of the sea-beach which is 120 km-long is sandy.

15 Minutes Ago

Lalakhal

Lalakhal,which is another top tourist attraction in Sylhet Division, is covered with hills, natural forests, tea gardens, and rivers under the Jainta Hill which comprises part of the Meghalaya Ranges of Bangladesh.

15 Minutes Ago

Tea Garden's

Madhabpur Tea Garden is located 5 kilometres to the South of Kamalganj upazila and about 50 kilometres from Moulvibazar Sadar district. Madhabpur artificial lake has added beauty to the tea garden.
